Ever resonated with a service or product so much that you could see it in your life? Felt like you needed it there?

Chances are they told you a story

Stories are instinctual. They are how humans have bonded and passed on information for millennia. They appeal to community instincts deep inside of us. Brands that tell a story and take their audience on a journey can tap into this. They become relatable, clear, unforgettable. But it isn’t always easy to understand how storytelling can be applied to companies. Most get it wrong.

The good news?

Stories are science as much as art. There is a structure that runs through them. Although popularised by Disney and used at the core of all of their productions, the seven point story framework is as old as time. Through a framework called brand script by Donald Miller, you can create a story for your company that makes the customer the hero and positions you as an essential guide on their journey.
